# Appearance
Jade has a predominantly citrus green body with a glade green belly and splotches of myrtle green along her back. Her wings match the color of her belly and fade out to alpine yellow.
# Abilities
Prior to her rebirth Jade had the ability to manipulate plants and fungi. She mainly used this power to grow the forests beyond the [Dragon Valley], which served as a natural separation between the land of the Vonn and the land of the dragons. After her rebirth in the liminal plane her powers were no longer accessible to her.
